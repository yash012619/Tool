How Our Image Compressor Works

Our advanced image compression algorithm reduces file size while maintaining visual quality. Here's what makes our tool special:

  • Smart Compression: Analyzes each image to apply optimal compression settings
  • Format Conversion: Convert between JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats
  • Privacy Focused: All processing happens in your browser - we never upload your images
  • Fast Processing: Compress images in seconds with our optimized algorithms

Best Practices for Image Compression

To get the best results from our image compressor:

  1. For photographs, use JPEG or WEBP format with 70-80% quality
  2. For graphics with transparency, use PNG or WEBP
  3. Test different compression levels to find the best balance of quality and file size
  4. Resize large images before compression for even smaller files
